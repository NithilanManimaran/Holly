Holly
A digital language interpreter
  • There are 357,000 deaf Canadians
  • There are 3.21 million hard of hearing Canadians
  • 1 in every 25 Canadians has impaired hearing

Holly is a service that is intended to make the lives of these people easier. Our service provides you an American Sign Language translation of an audio file or an English translation of a video in ASL.
